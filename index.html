IPS

IPS (International Primary School) of Wrocław is a well-regarded international school in Poland that provides high-quality education following international curricula, such as the Cambridge Primary programs. The school emphasizes a multicultural learning environment, catering to students from diverse backgrounds and promoting global citizenship. With a strong focus on i critical thinking, and language development, IPS Wrocław aims to nurture well-rounded individuals prepared for future academic and personal success.

High School

The High School building of IPS Wrocław is a modern school designed to support students in their final years of education. It provides a classrooms, science laboratories, and study spaces. With dedicated teachers, a strong emphasis on critical thinking, and various extracurricular opportunities, the High School building fosters both academic excellence and personal growth. Its welcoming atmosphere encourages students to develop independence, leadership skills, and a global perspective.
