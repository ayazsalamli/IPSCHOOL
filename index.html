IPS

IPS (International Primary School) of Wrocław is a well-regarded international school in Poland that provides high-quality education following international curricula, such as the Cambridge Primary and IB (International Baccalaureate) programs. The school emphasizes a multicultural learning environment, catering to students from diverse backgrounds and promoting global citizenship. With a strong focus on inquiry-based learning, critical thinking, and language development, IPS Wrocław aims to nurture well-rounded individuals prepared for future academic and personal success. Its modern facilities, experienced faculty, and student-centered approach make it a popular choice among expatriate and local families seeking an international education in Wrocław.

High School

The High School building of IPS Wrocław is a modern and well-equipped facility designed to support students in their final years of education. It provides an inspiring learning environment with state-of-the-art classrooms, science laboratories, and technology-integrated study spaces. The school follows internationally recognized curricula, such as the IB Diploma Programme, preparing students for university and future careers. With dedicated teachers, a strong emphasis on critical thinking, and various extracurricular opportunities, the High School building fosters both academic excellence and personal growth. Its welcoming atmosphere encourages students to develop independence, leadership skills, and a global perspective.
